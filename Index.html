THYME is an emerging fresh casual lifestyle concept that provides delicious, handcrafted meals made with locally sourced organic ingredients.

Chef Troi was trained in Italy working in Tuscany for 15 years prior to opening THYME in Vancouver and her food is best described as Italian/American fusion.
She believes in a farm to table approach and works closely with Maple Lane Farms in Abbotsford, B.C. for the freshest meat and Two EE’s Farms for locally sourced organic produce.

THYME operates from 7am – 11pm 7 days a week.
